Deep-Live-Cam is designed to be a productive tool for the AI-generated media industry. It helps artists animate custom characters, create engaging content, and prototype clothing or costume design on digital models. At the same time, the project team is fully aware that face-swapping technology can be misused, and has built both technical safeguards and explicit use requirements into the software. Using Deep-Live-Cam means accepting these terms.

## Intended use

The intended applications for Deep-Live-Cam are creative and productive:

- Animating custom or fictional characters in real time
- Creating entertainment content, memes, and short-form video
- Live shows, performances, and interactive presentations
- Clothing and costume design on digital models
- AI-generated media production where all parties consent

<Note>
  Deep-Live-Cam is designed for legal, consensual use. Deploying it to deceive, defraud, harass, or impersonate someone without their knowledge is a violation of this policy and, in most jurisdictions, a violation of the law.
</Note>

## Ethical use requirements

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Obtain consent when using a real person's face">
    If you use a photograph of a real person as the source face, you must obtain that person's explicit consent before using their likeness. This applies to public figures as well as private individuals.

    Using someone's face without consent to generate deepfake content — especially content that is embarrassing, defamatory, or sexual — is an ethical violation and may constitute identity theft, defamation, or non-consensual intimate imagery depending on your jurisdiction.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Label output as a deepfake when sharing online">
    Any output you create with Deep-Live-Cam and share publicly must be clearly identified as AI-generated or deepfake content. Presenting deepfake media as authentic footage — in news, social media, video calls, or any public context — is deceptive and potentially illegal.

    Practically, this means adding a visible label, caption, or watermark to videos and images before publishing them.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Content restrictions

Deep-Live-Cam includes a built-in content filter powered by [opennsfw2](https://github.com/bhky/opennsfw2) that automatically prevents the application from processing:

- Nudity and sexually explicit content
- Graphic violence and gore
- Sensitive material such as war footage

This filter runs before any face swap is applied. If the input content is classified as inappropriate, processing stops immediately. The filter is an intentional part of the software and is not a bug.

<Warning>
  Attempting to bypass or disable the NSFW filter violates this policy. The project maintainers reserve the right to add watermarks or shut down the project if legally required.
</Warning>
